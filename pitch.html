The Current Process

How coverage works right now

It works, but it's unintuitive and slow:

1

Massive emails go out

Someone needs coverage, so they send out a big email. Replies start coming in from people who may or may not actually be available.

2

Manual checking

Everyone has to pull up the schedule and cross-reference to see if they're even off that day before responding.

3

Leads piece it together

Leads have to track who offered, confirm everything, and document it—often across multiple threads.

2 / 6
The Prototype

Ops Scheduling

Upload the schedule, and it turns into an interactive dashboard. You can request coverage directly from your shift, it only surfaces people who are actually off, posts to a bulletin, and gives leads a clean approval queue.

Less noise. Less back-and-forth. A much clearer process for everyone.

The Flow

Three steps, no noise

1

Request

Click your shift, add a note if you want, submit. No massive emails—it just posts to the bulletin.

2

Offer

Only people who are actually off see it. One click to offer coverage. No manual checking required.

3

Approve

Lead sees who offered, approves it. Everyone gets notified. Done—no chasing threads.
